Easy Butter Chicken

Fancy a healthy version of your favourite Friday night chicken curry? The chicken can be marinaded the day before so you can get ahead on your prep.

Ingridients:

  • 500g skinless boneless chicken thighs.
      • For the marinade.
    • 1 lemon, juiced.
    • 2 tsp ground cumin.
    • 2 tsp paprika.
    • 1-2 tsp hot chilli powder.
    • 200g natural yogurt.
      For the curry.
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil.
    • 1 large onion, chopped.
    • 3 garlic cloves, crushed.
    • 1 green chilli, deseeded and finely chopped (optional).
    • Thumb-sized piece ginger, grated.
    • 1 tsp garam masala.
    • 2 tsp ground fenugreek.
    • 3 tbsp tomato purée.
    • 300ml chicken stock.
    • 50g flaked almonds, toasted.
      To serve (optional).
    • Cooked basmati rice.
    • Naan bread.
    • Mango chutney or lime pickle.
    • Fresh coriander.
    • Lime Wedges.

Steps:

  1. In a medium bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients with some seasoning.
  2. Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces and toss with the marinade.
  3. Cover and chill in the fridge for 1h or overnight.
  4. In a large, heavy saucepan, heat the oil.
  5. Add the onion, garlic, green chili, ginger and some seasoning.
  6. Fry on a medium heat for 10min or until soft.
  7. Add the spices with the tomato purée, cook for a further 2min until fragrant, then add the stock and marinaded chicken.
  8. Cook for 15min, then add any remaining marinade left in the bowl.
  9. Simmer for 5min, then sprinkle with the toasted almonds.
  10. Serve with rice, naan bread, chutney, coriander and lime wedges, if you like.
